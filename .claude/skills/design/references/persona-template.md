# Persona Template

Structure each persona file in `.claudius/design/personas/<role-slug>.md` using this template.

---

```markdown
# {Persona Name}

**Role:** {role title from product.md, e.g., "Marketing Manager", "Developer", "Admin"}

## Goals
1. {Primary goal — what they're trying to accomplish}
2. {Secondary goal}
3. {Tertiary goal}
4. {Optional: additional goals, up to 5}

## Pain Points
1. {Current frustration with existing solutions/workarounds}
2. {Time/effort wasted on manual process}
3. {Missing capability in current tools}
4. {Optional: additional pain points, up to 5}

## Current Workaround
{What they do today without this product — specific tools, manual processes, or nothing at all. Be concrete: "Uses a shared Google Sheet with 47 tabs" not "Uses spreadsheets."}

## Technical Sophistication
**Level:** {non-technical | somewhat technical | technical | developer}
**Comfort with:** {what tools/interfaces they're used to — e.g., "Comfortable with Slack, Google Workspace, basic Notion. Never used a CLI."}

## Context of Use
**Where:** {desk, mobile, on-the-go, in meetings, in the field}
**When:** {daily workflow, weekly check-in, occasional use, always-on}
**Device:** {laptop, phone, tablet, shared screen}
**Environment:** {quiet office, noisy commute, distracted multitasking}

## Surfaces Used
| Surface | Why | Frequency |
|---------|-----|-----------|
| {Web app} | {primary workspace for deep tasks} | {daily} |
| {Mobile} | {quick checks, notifications} | {several times/day} |

## Key Workflows
1. {Primary workflow — the thing they do most often, linked to flow file}
2. {Secondary workflow}
3. {Optional: additional workflows}
```
